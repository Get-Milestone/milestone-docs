# Hours Tracking

## What It Is
Automatic consumption of hours from Jira worklogs.

## Why MSPs Use It
- Accurate billing  
- Prevents over-consumption  
- Transparent reporting for clients  

## How It Works
- Hours log automatically  
- Edits update deltas  
- Deletions subtract time  
- Hours shown in Portal, Directory, Dashboards

## Tip
Review hours weekly to catch anomalies early.
