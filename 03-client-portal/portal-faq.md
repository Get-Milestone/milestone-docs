# Portal FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the Client Portal—organized by who's asking. Use this for quick reference when clients (or your team) have questions.

---

## Questions from Clients

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I access the portal?**

A: Click the portal link provided by your service provider. If you've already created an account, go directly to the portal URL and sign in.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**

A: No. The portal works in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). No downloads or installations required.

**Q: I lost my login information. What do I do?**

A: On the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password" to reset your password via email. If you don't remember your email, contact your service provider.

**Q: Can my colleagues access the portal too?**

A: Yes. Ask your service provider for the portal link. Each colleague creates their own account, and all will see the same company information.

---

### Submitting Requests

**Q: How do I submit a new request?**

A: Click "New Request" on your dashboard. Fill in the title, description, and select the urgency level. Click Submit. Your request is immediately created and tracked.

**Q: What should I include in my request?**

A: Be as specific as possible:
- Clear, descriptive title
- Detailed description of the issue or need
- Steps to reproduce (for bugs)
- Urgency level
- Any relevant screenshots or context

**Q: Can I attach files to my request?**

A: Currently, describe any relevant files in your message. Your service provider may request specific files through the messenger.

**Q: What do the urgency levels mean?**

| Level | Use When |
|-------|----------|
| **Low** | No time pressure, can wait |
| **Normal** | Standard priority, regular workflow |
| **High** | Business impact, needs attention soon |
| **Critical** | Blocking work, immediate attention needed |

### Tracking Requests

**Q: How do I check my request status?**

A: Go to Requests → Open Requests. Each request shows its current status and SLA timer.

**Q: What do the status labels mean?**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | Request received, awaiting triage |
| **In Progress** | Actively being worked on |
| **Completed** | Issue resolved, work finished |

**Q: What is the SLA timer?**

A: SLA (Service Level Agreement) timers show the committed time for response and resolution. Green means on track; yellow means approaching deadline; red means deadline passed.

**Q: Where do I see completed requests?**

A: Go to Requests → Closed Requests for your complete history.

---

### Communication

**Q: How do I communicate with the team?**

A: Open any request and use the Messages section to send a message. Replies appear instantly.

**Q: Will I be notified of updates?**

A: You'll see new messages when you log into the portal. Email notifications may be enabled depending on your configuration.

**Q: Can I reply to requests via email?**

A: Use the portal messenger for all request-related communication. This ensures everything is tracked and visible in one place.

---

### Hours and Billing

**Q: Where do I see my hours balance?**

A: On your dashboard home page, you'll see hours purchased, consumed, and remaining.

**Q: How are hours calculated?**

A: Hours are automatically tracked from the time your service provider logs working on your requests. This happens in real-time.

**Q: What happens when I run out of hours?**

A: Your service provider will contact you about purchasing additional hours. Service typically continues, but you'll need to discuss terms.

**Q: Can I see hours breakdown by request?**

A: Yes. Open any request to see hours logged against that specific work.

## Questions from Your Team

### Portal Setup

**Q: How do I generate a portal link?**

A: Client Directory → Portal Access tab → Select client → Configure options → Generate Link. Copy and share the link.

**Q: How long do portal links last?**

A: Default is 90 days. You can configure shorter or longer expirations when generating.

**Q: Can I regenerate a link?**

A: Yes. Generate a new link anytime. The new link works; the old link continues until its original expiration.

**Q: How do I disable portal access for someone?**

A: Currently, users retain access once they create an account. For urgent access removal, contact support.

### Troubleshooting Client Issues

**Q: Client says the link doesn't work**

Steps:
1. Check if link has expired
2. Generate a new link
3. Verify client is copying the full URL
4. Have them try a different browser

**Q: Client can't see their requests**

Steps:
1. Verify they're signed in
2. Check they accessed with the correct client's link
3. Ensure requests exist for their client ID
4. Have them refresh the page

**Q: Client sees another company's data**

This shouldn't happen. If it does:
1. Have client sign out immediately
2. Investigate which link they used
3. Contact support to correct account association

**Q: Messages not syncing**

Steps:
1. Refresh the portal page
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify message was sent from Milestone (not Jira comments)

### Best Practices Questions

**Q: Should all client contacts have portal access?**

Recommended: Yes, for anyone who:
- Submits requests
- Needs to track status
- Reviews hours consumption

Not necessary for:
- Executives who don't engage directly
- Technical users who prefer other channels

**Q: How do I encourage clients to use the portal?**

Tips:
- Reference portal in all communications
- Stop providing status via email ("check your portal")
- Highlight transparency benefits
- Show them the hours tracking feature

**Q: Should I share one link or individual links?**

One link per client company works. Multiple employees from the same company use the same link to create their accounts.

## Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**

A: Modern versions of:
- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari
- Microsoft Edge

Not supported: Internet Explorer

**Q: Does the portal work on mobile?**

A: Yes. The portal is responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.

**Q: Is the portal secure?**

A: Yes. The portal uses:
- HTTPS encryption
- Secure authentication
- Client-scoped data isolation
- No cross-client data exposure

**Q: Can the portal be embedded in our website?**

A: Yes. Portal links can be embedded via iFrame. See [Portal Links](portal-links.md) for embed code.
