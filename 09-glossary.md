# Glossary

**Client** – The company your MSP supports  
**Request** – A customer-submitted ticket  
**SLA** – Service Level Agreement  
**Response Time** – Time until first reply  
**Resolution Time** – Time until request resolved  
**Portal User** – End customer using portal  
**Hours Consumed** – Hours logged from Jira  
**Cloud ID** – Atlassian tenant ID  
**Client UUID** – Internal Milestone identifier
